<p>You could not view the password directly as it would be present under syslogins in an encrypted format.</p>

<p>But, You can reset the pwd.</p>

<p>Login to SSMS with "Windows Authentication" --> Expand "Security--> Logins"</p>

<p>Under "Logins", Select the appropriate LoginName and open the properties window. There you can Provide a new Password to your login.</p>
