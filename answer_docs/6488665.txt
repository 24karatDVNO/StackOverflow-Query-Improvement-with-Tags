<p>if you have any default PDF reader installed in the device/emulator then only you can open the <strong>PDF</strong> through intent but your application will not be a <strong>stand alone</strong> application. If you want to make your application stand alone  then you have to implement <strong>PDF</strong> reader in your application . There are <strong>open source project</strong> on <strong>PDF</strong> read your that can follow. </p>
