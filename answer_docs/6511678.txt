<p>Place your code in a function and call it whenever you make an AJAX call that has the potential to change the dimensions. If your AJAX calls are sporadic and for some reason you don't have control over them you can use <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/En/window.setInterval" rel="nofollow">setInterval</a> to check for changes continuously.</p>
