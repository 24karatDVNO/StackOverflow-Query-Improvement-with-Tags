<p>A simple way is </p>

<p><code>&lt;link rel="stylesheet" href="/css/master.css" type="text/css" media="&gt;</code></p>

<p>This needs to be placed in the <code>head</code> section of you page.</p>

<p>The <code>href</code> references the path to the file.</p>

<p>You can also use the <code>@import</code> method.</p>

<pre><code>&lt;style type="text/css"&gt;
  @import url("/css/master.css");
&lt;/style&gt;
</code></pre>
