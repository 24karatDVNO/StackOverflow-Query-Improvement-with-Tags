<p>If all you want to do is override the taxonomy term pages with a View, but NOT use the default view, you could create a custom module implementing <code>hook_menu()</code> or you could also take a look at the <a href="http://drupal.org/project/taxonomy_redirect" rel="nofollow">Taxonomy Redirect module</a>.  </p>

<p>From the Taxonomy Redirect page:</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>This module allows the administrator to change the destination of Taxonomy Term links.</p>
</blockquote>
