<ol>
<li>Open Xcode</li>
<li>File -> New Project</li>
<li>iOS or Mac App, you choose</li>
<li>Select the App Type (Master-Detail, etc) again, you choose</li>
<li>Choose Next</li>
<li>Give it a name, company identifier</li>
<li>Check the box next to "Use CoreData"</li>
<li>Choose Next</li>
<li>Find a home on the file system for your new app (~/Documents will work just fine)</li>
<li>Choose Create</li>
<li>Open [Your App Name]AppDelegate.m and notice that Xcode wrote all the coredata scaffolding for you!</li>
</ol>
