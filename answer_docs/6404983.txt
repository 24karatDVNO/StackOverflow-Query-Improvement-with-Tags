<p>If DropBox refuses to sync .git directory, there is a small work-around: set the environment variable <code>GIT_DIR</code> to <code>.git-meta</code> or some such.</p>

<p>I am currently using DropBox to hold my bare repository, and clone from it to my local storage on two machines. Aside from occasional pushes that I wouldn't normally do, it works reasonably well.</p>
