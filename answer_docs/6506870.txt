<p>Unless I'm very much mistaken, LOCAL <strong>accounts</strong> do not expire so there is no such attribute.  Account expiration is of value for domain level accounts when you need to terminate a relationship for an account after a given period.</p>

<p>Now if you're talking about local account password expirations,that's a whole different story, but I can't imagine why you'd want to delete local machine users based on expired passwords.</p>
