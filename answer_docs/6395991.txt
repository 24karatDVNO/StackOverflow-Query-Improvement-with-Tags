<p>if you want to add a single divider that will be the same for each row than that is simple. you just call setDivider(Drawable) on your list and add the drawable that will represent your divider.</p>

<p>If you want to divide the list by categories like divider then things get more complicated.</p>

<p>@CommonsWare has a nice demo and a library of how to achieve this in his cwac-Merge project.</p>

<p>here is a link: <a href="http://commonsware.com/cwac" rel="nofollow">Cwac</a></p>
