<p>In definition 1, <code>Dealer.ServiceCar&lt;T&gt;</code> takes a parameter of type <code>T</code>. You are passing <code>this</code> into the method, which is of type <code>Car&lt;T&gt;</code>. How are you going to convert <code>Car&lt;T&gt;</code> to <code>T</code>? The <code>where</code> constraint only says <code>T</code> is a <code>Car&lt;T&gt;</code>, but not the other way around.</p>
