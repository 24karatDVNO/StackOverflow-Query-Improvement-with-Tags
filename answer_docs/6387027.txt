<p>It's just a folder naming convention. You don't need to strictly adhere this, as long as you don't use folder names like <code>/tkjbwnkjh</code> instead of <code>/js</code> or <code>/scripts</code> or <code>/javascript</code>, etc. Keep it self-documenting so that future codebase maintainers won't rant on you.</p>
