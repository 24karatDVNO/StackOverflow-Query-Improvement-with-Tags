<p>Android does not support using ScrollViews or ListViews inside each other. It's possible to do, but it's A Bad Thing (TM), according to Google developers.</p>

<p>Search SO for similar problems with ListViews, and you'll find several discussions + suggestions on how to hack around the limitations of the OS. However, I would strongly suggest you reconsider your layout to try and find some other way to handle your layout.</p>
