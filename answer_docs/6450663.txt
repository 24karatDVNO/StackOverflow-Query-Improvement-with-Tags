<p>The concept is <em>universal quantification</em>, the relational operator is called <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Relational_algebra#Division_.28.C3.B7.29" rel="nofollow"><em>division</em></a>, popularly known as <a href="http://www.dbdebunk.com/page/page/772076.htm" rel="nofollow">"the supplier who supplies all parts"</a>. </p>

<p>Because you don't consider <code>ID = 5</code> to represent a duplicate, you should be looking at <a href="http://www.simple-talk.com/sql/t-sql-programming/divided-we-stand-the-sql-of-relational-division/" rel="nofollow">exact division</a> (i.e. without a remainder) and an empty divisior is probably not an issue in your case. </p>
