<p>First of all MVVM light is suitable for enterprise apps. However, it is a toolkit and not a full fetched framework, so if you need composition you will have to build it yourself. You could draw on MEF for support.</p>

<p>Prism on the other side is a fameworkthat builds on the MVVM model and the MEF. It has composition on board and you do not have to implement it on your own.</p>

<p>So it depends non then scope and the properties of your enterpise application which one you choose.</p>
