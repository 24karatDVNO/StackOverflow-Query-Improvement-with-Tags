<p>The a tag is an anchor tag so it needs something between the opening and closing tag to be the anchor text or in this case image.</p>

<p>Syntax for the anchor tag is</p>

<pre><code>&lt;a href="url/link"&gt;....&lt;/a&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>What ever you put where the ... is, is the anchor for the link being either text or image.</p>

<p>Some tags doen't have an opening and closing tag like the a tag.</p>

<pre><code>&lt;a href=""&gt;&lt;/a&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>Image tags simply close with the /> or > depending on which version of html you are writing.</p>

<pre><code>&lt;img src="" &gt;
</code></pre>
