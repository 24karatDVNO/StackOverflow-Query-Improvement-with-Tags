<p>this is a dataset technology.  it supports tabular data (either from a database or constructed in code), and you can then construct queries and filters against the dataset (and sort), all in-memory.  its fast and easy-to-use.  MOST IMPORTANTLY, you can perform queries against ANY column or attribute on the dataset.</p>
