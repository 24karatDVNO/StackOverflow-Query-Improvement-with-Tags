<p>Try wrapping your list content in divs like this:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;ul&gt;
    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;div&gt;
            Item 1
        &lt;/div&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;

    &lt;li&gt;
        &lt;div&gt;
            Item 2
        &lt;/div&gt;
    &lt;/li&gt;
&lt;/ul&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>And give your <code>&lt;ul&gt;</code> or containing element a fixed width, or <code>max-width</code> if you're feeling adventurous.</p>
