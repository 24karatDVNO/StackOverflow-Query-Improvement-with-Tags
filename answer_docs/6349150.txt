<p>Adding a tag to your page will not control the UI in the Internet Control Panel (the dialog that appears when you selection Tools -> Options).  If you're looking at your homepage which could be google.com, msn.com, about:blank or example.com, the Internet Control Panel has no way of knowing what the contents of your page may be, and it will not download it in the background.</p>

<p>Have a look at <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/dd567845%28v=vs.85%29.aspx" rel="nofollow">this document on MSDN</a> which discussed compatibility mode and how to turn it off for your site.</p>
