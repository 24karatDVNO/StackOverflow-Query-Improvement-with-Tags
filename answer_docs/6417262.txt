<p>In AS3.0 you can apply these styles to your components with some lines of code. This is an example from Adobe Help</p>

<pre><code>var tf:TextFormat = new TextFormat(); 
tf.color = 0x333333; 
tf.font = "Georgia"; 
tf.size = 24; 
tf.align = "center"; 
tf.italic = true; 

textInput.setStyle("textFormat", tf);
</code></pre>
