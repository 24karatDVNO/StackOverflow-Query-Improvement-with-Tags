<p>I also met this problem. I use <code>adobe dreameweaver cs4</code> (non english version)</p>

<p>I solve it as below: </p>

<ol>
<li><p>add <code>header('Content-type: text/html; charset=utf-8');</code> at the top of the PHP page file. </p></li>
<li><p><strong>IMPORTANT</strong>  In <code>adobe dreameweaver</code>, you should modify <code>Page Properties</code> from the <code>top menu</code>  <code>Modify (M) -&gt; Page Properties (P)</code>, choose <code>Title/coding</code> and modify <code>unicode</code> to <code>unicode (uft-8)</code> handly.</p></li>
</ol>
