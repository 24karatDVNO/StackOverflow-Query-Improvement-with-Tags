<p>Use the NSString function <code>componentsSeparatedByString</code>. For example, <code>NSString *url = @"http://www.google.com/1/2/3/test.gif"; NSArray *components = [url componentsSeparatedByString:@"/"];</code> would give you an NSArray of all strings in between a '/' character.</p>
