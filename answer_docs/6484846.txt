<p>Internet Explorer did not support border-radius until IE9, much to the frustration of developer and designers. With IE9, the important steps are using the edge META tag and provide the border radius:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;meta http-equiv="X-UA-Compatible" content="IE=edge" /&gt;
&lt;style&gt;
border-top-right-radius: 7px;
border-top-left-radius: 7px;
border-bottom-right-radius: 2px;
border-bottom-left-radius: 2px;
&lt;/style&gt;
</code></pre>
