<p>From what I understand, you are wanting a flexible design that elements will resize depending on the browser window?</p>

<p><strong>Take a look at this JSfiddle: <a href="http://jsfiddle.net/q62AM/1/" rel="nofollow">http://jsfiddle.net/q62AM/1/</a></strong> </p>

<p>*<em>Thanks to @Jared Farrish for putting together the jsfiddle for everyone to play with.</em></p>

<p>Basically, with flexible designs you need to use flexible increments such as percentages. You can set <code>min-width</code>s if you don't wish for your design to squash too small but you should consider setting the <code>min-width</code> on the parent container.</p>
