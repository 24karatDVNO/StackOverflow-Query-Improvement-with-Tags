<p>If you must store this much in session state, and you're limited on process memory, then it seems like the database would be the way to go.  Just make sure that everything you are putting in <code>Session</code> is serializable, since that will be required.  More details are on <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ms178586.aspx" rel="nofollow">MSDN</a>.  The database option is, of course, <code>SessionStateMode.SQLServer</code>.</p>
