<p>Using the Native UI helps the user experience of your end product.</p>

<p>Also, as phones are in general resource-light, by stripping away the "browser" layer, your end product is likely to be more responsive, and need less overhead in the form of data transmitted.</p>

<p>In terms of Android, I believe the strong allure is to hook into the <code>Intent</code> system.  It is very powerful and incredibly seamless.  Not sure about the iThings, have not developed for them yet.</p>
