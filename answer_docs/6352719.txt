<p>Output in Drupal is handled by the theme layer. Depending on what you're trying to ouput, you typically pass an array to a theme_function. For instance, if you want to output a link, you use <code>theme_link()</code>. api.drupal.org comes in very handy. An intro to the theme layer and a list of theme_functions lives here: <a href="http://api.drupal.org/api/drupal/modules--system--theme.api.php/group/themeable/7" rel="nofollow">http://api.drupal.org/api/drupal/modules--system--theme.api.php/group/themeable/7</a>. </p>
