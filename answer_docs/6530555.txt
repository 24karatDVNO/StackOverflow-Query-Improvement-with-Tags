<p>When you create a connection to a database (or other data source) formulas place in columns next to the imported data are adjusted on refresh to cope with this excat issue.  These formulas will typically be in the form <code>=SomeFunction([@ColumnName], ...)</code></p>

<p>Likewise Sums of the form <code>=SUM(ConnectionName[ColumnName])</code> will automatically be right when the connection is refreshed</p>
