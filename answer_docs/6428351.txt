<p>Yes, it is definitely possible. You would have to use the APIs exposed by Facebook and Twitter. You can then post let's say status on both twitter and facebook by calling update status related APIs of both Twitter and Facebook on the <code>apply</code> button click event.</p>

<p>You can use <a href="https://github.com/danielcrenna/tweetsharp" rel="nofollow">this</a> for Twitter and <a href="http://facebooksdk.codeplex.com/" rel="nofollow">this</a> for Facebook both are C# clients for Twitter and Facebook respectively.</p>
