<p>Since this is a fan page, you don't need to call an FQL query, since an FQL query requires that the user has first authorized your application.  Facebook will send a signed_request post parameter to your iframe fan page.  See <a href="https://stackoverflow.com/questions/5093398/check-if-user-liked-page/5100287#5100287">this answer</a> on how to decode that signed_request parameter and check to see if they are a fan of your page or not. </p>
