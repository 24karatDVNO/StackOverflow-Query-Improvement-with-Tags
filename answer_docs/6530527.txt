<p><strong>QUICK HELP</strong></p>

<p><kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Option</kbd> / <kbd>Alt</kbd> + <kbd>f</kbd> </p>

<p>Find the Quick help. For example,</p>

<pre><code>@property (nonatomic, retain) NSString *strQuery;
</code></pre>

<p>My Text cursor is at <code>NSString</code> &amp; if I press <strong><kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Option</kbd> + <kbd>f</kbd></strong> then It will show a quick documentation in the inspector/utilities.</p>
