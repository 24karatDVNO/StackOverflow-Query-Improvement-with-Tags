<p>To create a multi-lined Label of a Button:</p>

<ol>
<li>Skin your Button (Simplest way: Create Skin from the Design view)</li>
<li>In the Skin, scroll to where you see the <strong>Label</strong> with the id="<strong>labelDisplay</strong>"</li>
<li>Set a <strong>Fixed Width</strong> to that Label in the Skin &amp; you're Done!</li>
</ol>

<p>Once you populate the label property of the Button, it'll auto multi-line for you</p>
