<p>If you want to save the state of your activity and have it persist through multiple onCreate() calls, you can use the Bundle object. You can save to the Activity's Bundle in onSaveInstanceState() and then load it back up in onCreate(). Take a look at <a href="http://developer.android.com/reference/android/app/Activity.html" rel="nofollow">http://developer.android.com/reference/android/app/Activity.html</a> and <a href="http://developer.android.com/reference/android/os/Bundle.html" rel="nofollow">http://developer.android.com/reference/android/os/Bundle.html</a>. </p>
