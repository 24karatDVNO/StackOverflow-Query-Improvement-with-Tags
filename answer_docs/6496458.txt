<p>The app is responsible for sending it to your Push Server. You have to define your own method to do this, e.g. HTTP POST or some other proprietary protocol. </p>

<p>For example, Urban Airship's iOS library makes an HTTP request to its server. See the <code>registerDeviceTokenWithExtraInfo</code> method in the <a href="https://github.com/urbanairship/ios-library/blob/master/Airship/Library/AirshipLib/UAirship.m" rel="nofollow">UAirship.m</a> file.</p>
