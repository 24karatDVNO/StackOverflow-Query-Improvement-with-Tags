<p>Found the problem--it was in the copy protection setting.  I had it enabled, and apparently it's been known to cause some problems with app visibility on the market for certain devices.  All you have to do to fix it is disable the copy protection</p>

<p>Google recommends using their newer licensing service instead. <a href="http://developer.android.com/guide/publishing/licensing.html" rel="nofollow">http://developer.android.com/guide/publishing/licensing.html</a></p>
