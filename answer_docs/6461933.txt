<p>The thing is, vertical-align was mainly designed for specifying the behaviour of table-cells. Although the name suggests that any content shall be aligned in the middle, it simply does not.</p>

<p>You can find a very good article <a href="http://phrogz.net/css/vertical-align/index.html" rel="nofollow">here</a> about what vertical-align really is and how to achieve your intended purpose - aligning the text vertically inside your div.</p>
