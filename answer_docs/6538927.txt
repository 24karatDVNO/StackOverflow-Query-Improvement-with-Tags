<p>Open the pending changes conflicts dialog:</p>

<p>Top Menu -> View -> Other Windows -> Pending Changes</p>

<p>If you want to make your local version the current version.  You will be presented with a list of conflicts.  </p>

<p>For every conflict select: "Keep Local Version"</p>

<p>Here is an example of what your screen will look like notice the "Keep Local Version" option to the right of the item in question.</p>

<p><img src="https://i.stack.imgur.com/NIc6O.png" alt="enter image description here"></p>

<p>Check it in and you should be good to go.</p>
