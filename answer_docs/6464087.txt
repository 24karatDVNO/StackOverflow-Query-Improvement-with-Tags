<p>You could <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/ee330302.aspx" rel="nofollow">add validation visual states</a> to the control or <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.windows.controls.validation.errortemplate.aspx" rel="nofollow">apply a special ErrorTemplate</a> these could take care of visual requirements such as turning blue.</p>

<p>Setting a property or calling a method could be done by subscribing to <a href="http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.windows.controls.validation.error.aspx" rel="nofollow">the Error event</a> of the Validation class.</p>
