<p>There is no supported way to augment the Setup menu.</p>

<p>You should probably be using Custom Settings for what you are describing - they were designed for app-level and user-level configuration, and are available from Setup.</p>

<p>Otherwise, if it really is custom setup that requires fancier logic / UI / etc, you may want to do what many SFDC ISV's do and ship your app with its own configuration tab that's hidden, and/or custom VF page that manages app settings.</p>
