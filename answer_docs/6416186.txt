<p>You may write HTML codes like below to create a selected or unselected checkbox</p>

<pre><code>&lt;input type="checkbox" name="option1" value="1" checked="checked" /&gt;
&lt;input type="checkbox" name="option2" value="2" /&gt;
</code></pre>
