<p>I also use the explain plan. </p>

<p>The best method to tune a query is to know as much about what it's purpose is and what it is doing.</p>

<p>Learn about the tables, indexes, constraints, data most importantly, and how the CBO (or RBO) is going to retrieve the data.</p>

<p>You can use software to point and click your way through, but if you don't know yourself what the query and oracle is doing, you won't get very far.</p>
