<p>Since the cell is reusable, when scrolling, the cells outside the view port will enqueue, and the <code>-tableView:cellForRowAtIndexPath:</code> will dequeue the enqueued cells for reusing, the cells' property remain the status on enqueuing, so you need to set to new status.</p>

<p>to fix your problem, you need to remember the selected cell's indexPath or other things can identity the cell, and set the accessoryType for it in <code>-tableView:cellForRowAtIndexPath:</code>, do not rely on the <code>-tableView:didSelectRowAtIndexPath:</code>.</p>
