<p>The exception is indicating that the XML should be namespace qualified like:  </p>

<pre><code>&lt;ObjectProp type="single"&gt;
   &lt;MapProp xmlns="http://localhost/txt/1.12/obj/domain" sn="79" version="7.0"&gt;
      ...
   &lt;/MapProp&gt;
&lt;/ObjectProp&gt;
</code></pre>
