<p>If you mean that you want to call a function when you click some link, then you can easily do this:</p>

<p><code>&lt;a href="javascript:your_function_name();"&gt;Click Me&lt;/a&gt;</code></p>

<p>or</p>

<p><code>&lt;a href="#" onclick="your_function_name(); return false;"&gt;Click Me&lt;/a&gt;</code></p>
