<p>The problem is on <code>&lt;body onload="initialize(&lt;%= @segnalazione.id %&gt;,&lt;%= @segnalazione.dove %&gt;); "&gt;</code> if <code>@segnalazione.dove</code> expands to <code>(lat, lng)</code> the comma operator inside the parentheses will make <code>(lat, lng)</code> evaluate to <code>lng</code>. You can't use "()" how you seen to use it, go for <code>{lat: lat, lng: lng}</code>(as an object) or <code>[lat, lng]</code>(as a array).</p>
