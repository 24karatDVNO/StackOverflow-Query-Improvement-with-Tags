<p>You can have a look at the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Levenshtein_distance" rel="nofollow">Levenshtein distance</a>, the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Needleman%E2%80%93Wunsch_algorithm" rel="nofollow">Needleman–Wunsch algorithm</a> and the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Damerau%E2%80%93Levenshtein_distance" rel="nofollow">Damerau–Levenshtein distance</a></p>

<p>They give you metrics evaluating the amount of differences between two strings (ie the numbers of addition, deletion, substitution, etc.). They are often used to measure the variation between DNA.</p>

<p>You will easily find implementations in various languages.</p>
