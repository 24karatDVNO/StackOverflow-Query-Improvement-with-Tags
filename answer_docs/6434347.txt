<p>If you use <code>text</code> as the field type, then eat, eats, eaten and eating will all be stored as <code>eat</code> and a search for <code>FieldName:eat</code> will find all of them. If you change the field type to <code>text-gen</code> then the search for <code>FieldName:eat</code> will only find "eat", not eats, eaten or eating.</p>
