<p>You can change the background colour with:</p>

<p><code>element.style.backgroundColor = "#ff0000";</code></p>

<p>Where <code>element</code> is a variable containing a DOM element (so in your case, <code>max1box</code> or <code>max2box</code>).</p>

<p>So you just need to add that into your function to change the colour of the appropriate input, and then use a similar line with a different colour to change it again.</p>
