<p>Just use HTTPs. Whether its a "traditional" form or XHR SSL\HTTPS is possible. </p>

<p>Note that, with something like firebug (or Chromes debuger's network tab), you'll still be able to see the data... but that's because its part of the browser. An "external" sniffer, like Wireshark or a potential bad guy doing a man-in-the-middle attack, will not see the plain-text data on the wire (but will see encrypted data instead).</p>
