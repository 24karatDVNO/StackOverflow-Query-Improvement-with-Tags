<p>You could create custom RouteConstraints by implementing the <code>IRouteConstraint</code>. Within the match method you could add the logic for the datetimes you want to check. This would require you to have multiple routes in your routetable. which all refer to different controllers/actions where you can show a different View to the user.</p>
