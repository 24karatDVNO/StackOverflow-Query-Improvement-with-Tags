<p>There are many SOAP server implementations for python, some more usable than others, search for packages related to SOAP at <a href="http://pypi.python.org/pypi?%3Aaction=search&amp;term=soap&amp;submit=search" rel="nofollow">PyPI</a> or take a look at the wiki page about <a href="http://wiki.python.org/moin/WebServices" rel="nofollow">web services</a> at python.org. There are essentially two types of SOAP servers for python:</p>

<ol>
<li>Servers that can generate server stubs from WSDL files (like ZSI)</li>
<li>Servers that can produce WSDL from the service class methods directly (like soaplib, ladon)</li>
</ol>
