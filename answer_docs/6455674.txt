<p><code>instance 20</code> is probably an unnamed container inside the scroll pane that Flash masks and scrolls.  According to the docs, ScrollPane.content is a reference to the display object you assign using ScrollPane.source.  Given that, <code>regFormSP.content</code> should be your form, and not child thereof.</p>
