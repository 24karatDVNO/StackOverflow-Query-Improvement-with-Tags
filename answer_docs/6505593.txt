<p>When you configure your transactions via @Transactional, then you should handover the configuration of your transactions to the annotation.<br/>
Here you start your transaction and then hope that the @Transactional will also be triggerd.<br/>
for more information you would best start reading <a href="http://static.springsource.org/spring/docs/2.5.x/reference/transaction.html" rel="nofollow">http://static.springsource.org/spring/docs/2.5.x/reference/transaction.html</a> => 9.5.6. Using @Transactional</p>
