<p>An alternative to a scheduled job/SSIS solution would be to create a <em>view</em> in <strong>Destination</strong> that maps back to <strong>Source</strong> via a <em>linked server</em> and <em>openrowset</em> call.  This way you don't really need to move the data, and can access it when you like in real-time from <strong>Source</strong>.</p>
