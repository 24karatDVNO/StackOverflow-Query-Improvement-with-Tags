<p>Some times it is necessary (or at least more convenient) to modify the ArrayList (e.g., is a field of some other class, or it is being modified by other thread that does not know about the adapter). </p>

<p>In those cases, you will need to call <a href="http://developer.android.com/reference/android/widget/BaseAdapter.html#notifyDataSetChanged%28%29" rel="nofollow">adapter.notifyDataSetChanged()</a></p>
