<p>For analyzing log files of remote machines you can always use <a href="http://commons.apache.org/vfs/filesystems.html#SFTP" rel="nofollow">Apache Commons sftp</a> programmatically to FTP a copy of the remote log file to your PC.</p>

<p>If you configure the log files to be rotatable or to rotate each time they reach a specific size, you can avoid reloading the same information over and over. </p>
