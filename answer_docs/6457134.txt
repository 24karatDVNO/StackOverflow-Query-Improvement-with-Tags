<p>You have to use an image to change the actual size or form of the bullet itself:</p>

<p>You can use a background image with appropriate padding to nudge content so it doesn't overlap:</p>

<pre><code>list-style-image:url(bigger.gif);
</code></pre>

<p>or</p>

<pre><code>background-image: url(images/bullet.gif);
</code></pre>
