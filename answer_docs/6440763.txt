<p>You would put the full image within an attribute of the thumbnail, for instance <code>data-full</code>. Then you would bind a mouseover event to the thumbnail that will place the image from <code>data-full</code> within a target div and remove it again on mouseout.</p>

<p>Or you could place the images there in advance, and just bind a mouseover event to the thumbnail that will <code>show()</code> the related full image.</p>
