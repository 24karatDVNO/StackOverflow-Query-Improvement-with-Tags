<p>This would probably be a good candidate for a database task, or even a flatfile log record at the very least. Emails are not reliable enough to handle the task of keeping track of your data.  Sure, you get the info by email, but what then? You probably have or will have other steps to take with that data, the fact that it is in an email will not make it easy to automate those tasks.  </p>

<p>If you lose your emails, you lose your data. Ok .. backing up emails is no harder that backing up a database, but the database is built to keep track of data, whereas emails are not. </p>
