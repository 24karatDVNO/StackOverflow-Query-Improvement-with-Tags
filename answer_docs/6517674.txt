<p>If you modify your view, there's a section 'Style' that you can expand. Selecting 'Preview Pane' as the style gives you a view with a left column showing the title and a right column displaying the other columns (vertically) of the hovered item.</p>

<p>I don't know if this is exactly what you need, but it's very easy to configure and doesn't need any modifications with SharePoint Designer, so you may want to take a look at it.</p>
