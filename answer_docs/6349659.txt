<p>If you're doing non-unit testing, <a href="http://testng.org" rel="nofollow">TestNG</a> might be a better option than JUnit. More specifically, data providers seem like they would be a good fit in the scenario you describe (<a href="http://testng.org/doc/documentation-main.html#parameters-dataproviders" rel="nofollow">example here</a>). Data providers can retrieve their data pretty much from anywhere: a text/properties/xml/Excel file, a database, a remote host, etc...</p>
