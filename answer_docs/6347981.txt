<p>You can access the thumbnail class to perform fade in or out.</p>

<p>$(.thumbnail).fadeIn('slow');</p>

<p>or</p>

<p>$(.thumbnail).fadeIn(700); // miliseconds</p>

<p>to fadeout do the reverse</p>

<p>$(.thumbnail).fadeOut('fast');</p>

<p>or</p>

<p>$(.thumbnail).fadeOut(300); // miliseconds</p>
