<p>When an iframe of domainY is included on a page of domain X, the cookies set within the iframe are considered third-party cookies.</p>

<p>Therefore, a higher percentage of users block those cookies by default. Specifically, iOS and Safari block third party cookies by default, and other users block them on an optional basis. Third-party cookies are less reliable for data for this reason.</p>
