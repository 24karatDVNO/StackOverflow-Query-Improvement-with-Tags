<p>You could look at the implementation of the visualization that is integrated in the Neo4j Server. That uses D4 and javascript (actually coffeescript) to render the graph.</p>

<p>Sources are here: <a href="https://github.com/neo4j/community/tree/master/server" rel="nofollow">https://github.com/neo4j/community/tree/master/server</a></p>

<p>You can also use <a href="http://vis.stanford.edu/protovis/ex/force.html" rel="nofollow">protoviz</a> or other visualization <a href="http://wiki.neo4j.org/content/Visualization_options_for_graphs" rel="nofollow">tools</a> for that.</p>
