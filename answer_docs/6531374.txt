<p>As you will have to handle collections of data, like your user list or the list of messages in a chat, you will have to use a control, you can databind the collection to. Therefore you need a control deriving from <em>ItemsControl</em>. There are <em>ListBox</em> or <em>ListView</em> (extended ListBox) or <em>DataGrid</em> for your purpose. Which to go for depends on what you want to do in detail regarding styling, selection of rows, etc.</p>

<p>All of these are suitable in general, so you will just have to do a bit of research to find out, which one is best for what you want to achieve.</p>
