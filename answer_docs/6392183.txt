<p>Another side-effect of not checking whether it's an image or not is when, and if, you need to display it somewhere on the website (in user profile, on front page, etc) then if it's not really an image, it will appear broken, and thus, your site will appear broken.</p>

<p>As for malware being injected, that depends on your server configuration. If it hasn't been updated to secure holes and exploits then yes, malware can infiltrate through (even though that's rare, but possible).</p>
