<p>It depends a lot on what you actually want to do. </p>

<p>If you want a kind of highlight for the checkbox input as such, you might style the label/text/wrapping element instead of the checkbox itself, i.e.:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;label id="abc"&gt;&lt;input type="checkbox" value="False" id="abc" /&gt;Bla&lt;/label&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>If you want to style the actual checkmark box, you may need to use a kind of skinning as shown here for example:</p>

<p><a href="http://ryanfait.com/resources/custom-checkboxes-and-radio-buttons/" rel="nofollow">http://ryanfait.com/resources/custom-checkboxes-and-radio-buttons/</a></p>

<p>Anyway this issue might heavily depend on the browser.</p>
