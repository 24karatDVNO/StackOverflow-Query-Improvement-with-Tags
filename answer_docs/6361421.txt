<p>For storing documents you could look into a Document-Oriented Database or Document Management System.</p>

<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Document_oriented_database" rel="nofollow">Document-Oriented Database</a></p>

<p>You can also look at other NoSQL databases that may make more sense than a typical RDBMS that are XML based.</p>

<p><a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/XML_database" rel="nofollow">XML Database</a></p>

<p>Yet another option is utilizing an RDBMS for document storage which allows you to organize documents by record and perform industry standard SQL queries to search for and retreive documents.</p>
