<p>You can use either <code>height: 'auto'</code> or <a href="http://www.trirand.com/jqgridwiki/doku.php?id=wiki%3aoptions" rel="nofollow">scrollOffset:0</a> jqGrid options to fix the problem. The reason is the way how the grid width will be calculated. It is not perfect and always reserves some additional place for the scroll bar.</p>

<p>Another options like <code>autowidth:true</code> or <code>shrinkToFit:false</code> can be also used in some situations.</p>
