<p>IE9 support <code>border-radius</code> property, as well as Fx and Chrome. Use unprefixed name.</p>

<p><a href="https://stackoverflow.com/questions/635851/support-for-border-radius-in-ie">Support for &quot;border-radius&quot; in IE</a></p>

<p>For older browsers that do not support even prefixed names, you can only use hacks as tables with images or so.</p>
