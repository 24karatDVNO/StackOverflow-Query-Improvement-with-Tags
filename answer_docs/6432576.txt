<p>First, I hope you're using Flex for more than just playing a video as that would be very inefficient.  Second, you should be using <a href="http://help.adobe.com/en_US/FlashPlatform/reference/actionscript/3/flash/media/StageVideo.html" rel="nofollow">StageVideo</a> to play videos on mobile devices since it's hardware accelerated. You should look at the some of the <a href="http://www.adobe.com/devnet/flashplayer/articles/stage_video.html" rel="nofollow">tutorials from Adobe</a> on how to use it on devices.</p>
