<p>Found something that could be useful </p>

<p>In info.plist:</p>

<pre><code>&lt;key&gt;UILaunchImageFile&lt;/key&gt;
&lt;string&gt;Default&lt;/string&gt;
&lt;key&gt;UILaunchImageFile~ipad&lt;/key&gt;
&lt;string&gt;Default_iPad&lt;/string&gt;
</code></pre>

<p>Then you need 2 files: Default.png (640x960 landscape) and Default_iPad.png (768x1024 landscape).</p>
