<p>In database systems, <em>normalize</em> doesn't generally mean "replace text with an id number". It would be pretty surprising if Google did that on the fly. Google generally follows the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Principle_of_least_astonishment" rel="nofollow">Principle of Least Surprise</a>.</p>

<p>Not only would it be surprising, it might require a join for every text column. That doesn't sound wise, does it?</p>
