<p>To understand how the binary layout of floating point variables works, I recommend you to read the <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IEEE_754-2008" rel="nofollow">wikipedia article</a> of the according standardization.</p>

<p>In a nutshell, all floating point numbers (<code>float</code>, <code>double</code>, <code>long double</code>, respectively implementations of <code>half</code>) consist of a mantisse and an exponent to represent the number.</p>
