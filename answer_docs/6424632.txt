<p>I would not call a JSP when using AJAX since JSP are best suited for viewing purposes (i.e. rendering HTML, or XML).</p>

<p>I would suggest you using a plain servlet. <a href="http://www.ibm.com/developerworks/library/j-ajax1/" rel="nofollow">Here's a simple tutorial on how to use AJAX with plain Servlets</a>.</p>

<p>If you still want to call JSP with your AJAX request you can still do that. Just point your AJAX request to your proper JSP URL.</p>
