<p>First, make sure your Scheme is set to Release.  Then, Build your app.  If all goes well with no errors, you should be able to get your release build of your app in your Derived Data directory (this is the default Xcode 4 location).</p>

<p>If you don't see anything in Derived Data, check under Xcode->Preferences and go to the Locations tab.  That will show you where your builds are going.</p>
