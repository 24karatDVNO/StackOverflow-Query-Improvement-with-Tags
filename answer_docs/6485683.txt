<p>An ActiveX control is based on COM, which uses refcounting. Your control should dispose its resources when it's refcount goes to zero. This is not trivial to detect from a C# activex control, but according to <a href="https://stackoverflow.com/questions/2223147/is-it-possible-to-intercept-or-be-aware-of-com-reference-counting-on-clr-object">this question</a>'s answer, it is doable.</p>
