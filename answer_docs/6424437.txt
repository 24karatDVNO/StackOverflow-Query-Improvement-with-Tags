<p>The actual question here is why are you using <code>alt</code> the way you are? If your image is simply decorative, you should have an empty <code>alt</code> attribute. look at the W3CS definition of the <code>alt</code> attribute.</p>

<p><a href="http://www.w3.org/QA/Tips/altAttribute" rel="nofollow">http://www.w3.org/QA/Tips/altAttribute</a></p>
