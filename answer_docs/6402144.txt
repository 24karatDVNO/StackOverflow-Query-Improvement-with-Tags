<p>As a start, check your Apache access and error logs and see if you find something fishy over there:</p>

<blockquote>
  <p><strong>How to debug errors?</strong> </p>
  
  <p>Once Apache
  starts, it will create two log files,
  the access_log and the error_log
  /<em>installdir</em>/apache2/logs directory.
  You can change these files to see the
  exact error in the application.</p>
</blockquote>
