<p>There is not default constructor for <a href="http://developer.android.com/reference/android/view/View.html#View%28android.content.Context%29" rel="nofollow">View objects</a>.  Take a look at the javadoc for <a href="http://download.oracle.com/javase/1,5.0/docs/api/java/lang/Class.html#newInstance%28%29" rel="nofollow">Class.newInstance()</a>.  It throws an <code>InstantiationException</code> if no matching constructor is found.</p>
