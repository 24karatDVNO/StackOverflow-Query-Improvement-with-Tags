<p>According to <a href="http://developer.android.com/reference/android/view/ViewGroup.LayoutParams.html" rel="nofollow">http://developer.android.com/reference/android/view/ViewGroup.LayoutParams.html</a>, the dimensions can either be <code>fill_parent</code>, <code>wrap_content</code> or a specific number of pixels. you can use <code>Display.getWidth()</code> and <code>Display.getHeight()</code> and pass in half of that to LayoutParams.</p>

<p>I'm not sure if theres a way to do that directly in the XML, though that would be much more elegant.</p>
