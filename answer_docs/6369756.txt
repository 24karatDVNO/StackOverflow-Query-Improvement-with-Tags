<p>I had a similar problem with the icon size and zoom level in my application. What I ended up doing was having 2 sets of overlays containing the markers, one with a "zoomed in" icon and one with a "zoomed out" icon. Then just changed the overlay at a certain zoom level (using a zoom poller - <a href="https://stackoverflow.com/questions/2013443/on-zoom-event-for-google-maps-on-android/5345237#5345237">On zoom event for google maps on android</a>)</p>
