<p>Getting <a href="http://download.oracle.com/javase/6/docs/api/java/lang/System.html#currentTimeMillis%28%29" rel="nofollow">System.currentTimeMillis</a> will get your system time to the millisecond. If you want to do a Swing event intermittently, such as displaying the current time in a stop watch or a simple animation, then a <a href="http://download.oracle.com/javase/tutorial/uiswing/misc/timer.html" rel="nofollow">Swing Timer</a> would work well. </p>
