<p>If your cookies are just constants that are not bound to a time, IP address, User-Agent string or anything that could be used to determine one computer from another, then anyone who could read them could send them unmodified to your app, and it will not be able to tell the difference between you and that guy. This is just as easy as sending the same <code>Cookie</code> header, which is a trivial task for anyone who could be interested in doing it.</p>

<p>I would recommend you reading some articles on securing HTTP sessions first. PHP has a lot of examples on how to properly do it. (Unfortunately, it also has a lot of <em>bad</em> examples, so be careful.)</p>
