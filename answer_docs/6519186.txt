<p>I solved this problem thanks to the viewport meta like that :</p>

<pre><code>&lt;meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0, user-scalable=no, minimum-scale=1.0, maximum-scale=1.0;" /&gt; 
</code></pre>

<p>After that a simple "body{width:100%}" works fine !</p>

<p>And it's compatible with other phones.</p>
