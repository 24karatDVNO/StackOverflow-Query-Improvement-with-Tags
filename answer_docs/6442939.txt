<p>In java, the <a href="http://download.oracle.com/javase/6/docs/api/java/lang/Comparable.html" rel="nofollow">Comparable</a> interface is meant exactly for that. For a "canonical example", have a look into the the <code>java.lang.Integer</code> class and see the implementation of <code>equals()</code>, <code>hashCode()</code> and <code>compareTo()</code>. Also, have a look at the JavaDoc info available for these methods in <code>java.lang.Object</code> and <code>java.lang.Comparable</code>.</p>
