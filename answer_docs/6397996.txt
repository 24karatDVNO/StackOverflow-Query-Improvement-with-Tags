<p>You can use <a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/String/substr" rel="nofollow"><strong><code>String.prototype.substr</code><em></strong><sup>[docs]</sup></em></a> (or <code>substring</code>) and an unary <code>+</code> to convert the string to a number:</p>

<pre><code>var number = +str.substr(1);
</code></pre>
