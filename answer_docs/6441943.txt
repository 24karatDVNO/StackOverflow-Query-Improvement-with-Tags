<p>You must use the <a href="http://developers.facebook.com/docs/guides/mobile/#android" rel="nofollow">Facebook API</a>, and get the image URLs through the <a href="http://developers.facebook.com/docs/reference/api" rel="nofollow">Graph API</a>.
